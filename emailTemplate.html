💸 Waiver Details

🎓 Eligible graduates can receive up to 60% tuition waiver based on their CGPA. Here's the breakdown:

🎯 CGPA (out of 4.00) 🏷️ Waiver Percentage
3.90+ 60%
3.85 – 3.89 50%
3.75 – 3.84 40%
3.50 – 3.74 35%
3.25 – 3.49 30%
3.00 – 3.24 25%
Below 3.00 20%

📝 Minimum SGPA of 3.00 and at least 12 credits are required to be eligible for the waiver.
